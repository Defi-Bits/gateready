Our Story

Stadium Solutions,
Built in Atlanta.

GateReady™ was born out of a simple frustration: showing up to a stadium with the wrong bag and getting turned away. We built the bag we wished existed — the exact size, the right material, at a price that makes sense.

Built in Atlanta, GA — home of Mercedes-Benz Stadium, State Farm Arena, Truist Park, and Auld Lang Syne Field. We know game day.

With FIFA World Cup 2026 bringing 11 US host cities — including Atlanta as a semifinal venue — GateReady™ is positioned to be the clear bag of choice for millions of fans.
